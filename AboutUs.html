In 2019

we took a significant step by moving to the esteemed IIT Madras incubation cell, where we began working with a fresh team of passionate individuals who shared our dedication to creating a more inclusive world through enhanced mobility. This journey has been marked by continuous innovation and transformation. 

In 2020

we introduced YALI 1.0, our first working prototype, which was met with enthusiasm and positive feedback from our users and supporters. Today, with the development of our groundbreaking YALI 3.0 modular electric vehicle platform, we're on the cusp of opening new possibilities and enhancing the lives of underserved market segments.
